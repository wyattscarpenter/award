The Carpenter Award

The Carpenter Award is an annual award awarded to the most promising people in the world. The Carpenter Award attempts to cover some of the expenses of each recipient in the hopes that freed of this burden they will go on to produce great work. The Carpenter Award can be awarded to any number of people, limited only by the yearly endowment.
